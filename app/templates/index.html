How To List Your Business

1. Visit our business submission page to list your business. You can choose the package that’s most suitable for your business promotion requirements.

2. Enter business name, address and contact details. Your listing will achieve the best results if you enter original text in the description field.

3. Please use the review option to make sure all details on your listing are correct. There is an option to review details before completing the submission.

4. Once you’ve finished checking that all details are correct, complete your submission. Pressing the ‘Submit’ button will put your listing in line for approval.

5. Submissions will be approved if they meet our requirements. Free listings usually go live in about 34-48 hours. Paid listings go live immediately.
