FAQ

Frequently asked questions

Q. How can I submit my business?

You can submit your business by registering your business with the required details. We provide you a review option in case you find any errors for correction. Then you have an option called final submission. Press that button, your business will get submitted. It is an instant process, can be done within few minutes.

Q. How much time will it take for my business to get approves?

Approval is given only if the business submission meets all the requirements. Usually, free submissions are approved after 24 to 48 hours whereas the paid listings go live within 12 hours.

Q. Can we share our business on social media?

Once your business gets submitted, we generate a unique URL for your business which helps to promote your business in all the social media platforms.

Q. How you help us in promoting our website?

We promote your business by indulging your business in free advertising and online exposure in all the social media platforms.

Q. How to get featured on the top of the listings?

We have Business & Premium packages through which your business gets featured on the top of the business listings within 6 hours.

Q. How to upgrade my free listings?

You can upgrade your listings by using our paid packages. The payment link: https://www.addbiz4u.com/pricing/

Q. Do you use cookies?

Yes, we do use cookies, but unless you enable it, we won’t have access to your browser history.

Q. What kind of breach procedures you follow?

We have an internal reporting system that helps us to improve our site’s performance.

Q. What are all the information that you get from us?

The personal data we collect is the comments provided by the users, cookies, and the images related to business.

Q. How long will you retain our information?

Your data will be stored in our database until you contact us to remove it.

Q. Do you receive data from third parties?

Yes, we do receive data from third parties. We advertise your business in third party sites like Facebook, Google, etc.

Q. How you protect our data?

We have a reliable SSL certified data system that secures your data.

Q. Do you have a comment session?

Yes, we do have a comment session below. We allow the users to share their opinions and view about the business that helps you in developing your business.

Q. What if we disagree with the terms and conditions?

You can better discontinue from the website if you disagree with our terms of use and privacy policy.
