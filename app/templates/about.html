About Us

Welcome to Addbiz4u

We welcome you to our free business directory which provides you the opportunity of submitting your business online from every Indian state. Here, you can add all the details related to your company, like the contact information, website address and other important points related to your product and services.

The Addbiz4u business directory helps business owners promote their local businesses online. Along with that, it can also help in finding a local business according to a customer’s preferences from all over India. You can find businesses of your type after submitting your business on this platform. All the listing and promotions over here are done free of cost. There will be a paid version of this service too, which will basically give you quick results. It will be embedded with some advanced tools and techniques.

Our Mission

Our mission at ADDBIZ4U is to increase the online visibility of local businesses. Why it is important? We have seen that these owners are not even able to create a website of their own. We all know the importance of online visibility for the growth of a business now a day. It is possible for a person living in a specific city to know about the local businesses of his city. However, a tourist won’t be able to do the same. This is one of the major reasons for which business listing is important.

With ADDBIZ4U, this has become very easy and comfortable to list your business online. If we talk about the opposite way, you don’t even need to fill the city name. Just open www.addbiz4u.com and allow your GPS to open. It will tell you where you are. You can find the best store, restaurant, malls, and places of entertainment etc. with the help of it. You can select the best places for you after reading the reviews mentioned.

ADDBIZ4U wants to list every small and local business online and also to help those who are in search of such businesses. Any kind of feedback and suggestions are welcome. We will love to hear it from you and will try to do improvements, if required.
